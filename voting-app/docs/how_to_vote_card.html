How to Vote

Office Bearer Election

Before you vote

  1. Sign the attendance register when you arrive.
  2. Wait for the chairman to open the meeting.
  3. You'll be handed a paper ballot and a slip with a six-character code and a QR code for voting on your phone.
You vote one way only - paper or phone, whichever you prefer.

Paper ballot

  1. Tick the box next to the candidates you choose, up to the number the office allows ("select 2" means tick up to two).
  2. Fold the card and hand it in for counting.

Phone

  1. Use the code slip - it tells you which WiFi to connect to, and has a QR code to scan with your phone's camera.
  2. Tick candidates for each office, up to the allowed number. Tap Cast Your Vote.
  3. The confirmation screen lets you know your vote has been registered.
More information? Speak to your office bearer or scan the QR.
